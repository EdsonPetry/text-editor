One Piece is a long-running Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. 
It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man whose body gains the properties of rubber after he accidentally eats a supernatural “Devil Fruit.” His dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary 
treasure known as the One Piece. The story takes place in a world covered by vast seas full of mysterious islands, powerful marines, rival pirates, and hidden secrets.
